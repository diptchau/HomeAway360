Overview

The villa overlooks the beautiful Vlicha Bay. Offering everything you need for a peaceful, relaxing holiday, while providing the perfect base for touring the fascinating island of Rhodes. The villa offers light and airy air-conditioned accommodation for up to 8 people, with 3 double bedrooms, all with sea views, living room, 2 bathrooms, outside dining area and its own swimming pool, with sun beds and parasols, as well as poolside barbecue. Villa Caterina also has wifi. The historic town of Lindos is 5 minutes away by car.
